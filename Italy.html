Italy

Basic Information

Capital: Rome

Official Language: Italian

Currency: Euro (EUR)

Population: Approx. 60 million

Geography

Italy is a European country known for its rich history, stunning coastlines, and beautiful mountains. It is bordered by France, Switzerland, Austria, and Slovenia.

Major Cities

  • Rome: The capital city, known for ancient ruins like the Colosseum.
  • Milan: A global fashion hub and financial center.
  • Venice: Famous for its canals and gondolas.
  • Florence: Known for Renaissance art and architecture.
  • Naples: A city rich in history and the birthplace of pizza.

Famous Landmarks

  • Colosseum: A massive ancient amphitheater in Rome.
  • Leaning Tower of Pisa: A famous tilted tower in Pisa.
  • Venice Grand Canal: The main waterway of Venice.
  • Florence Cathedral: A masterpiece of Renaissance architecture.
  • Amalfi Coast: A stunning coastline known for its scenic beauty.
