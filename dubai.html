Dubai

Basic Information

Capital: Dubai

Official Language: Arabic

Currency: United Arab Emirates Dirham (AED)

Population: Approx. 3.5 million

Geography

Dubai is a city in the United Arab Emirates, located on the southeast coast of the Persian Gulf. It is known for its desert climate, modern architecture, and luxurious lifestyle.

Major Landmarks

  • Burj Khalifa: The tallest building in the world, standing at 828 meters.
  • Palm Jumeirah: A man-made island shaped like a palm tree.
  • Burj Al Arab: A luxury hotel known for its sail-shaped design.
  • Dubai Mall: One of the largest shopping malls in the world.
  • Dubai Fountain: A spectacular choreographed water fountain show.
